Why Choose Beit-El Tibeb

We combine cultural authenticity, expert leadership, and innovative educational methods to preserve and share the richness of Ethiopian-Israeli heritage. Our programs are designed to empower individuals with traditional knowledge while using modern platforms to reach and unite our community worldwide.

About Our Institute

Beit-El Tibeb is a global center dedicated to empowering Ethiopian-Israeli culture, heritage, and art. We serve as a living bridge between worlds, offering authentic experiences through traditional crafts, culinary arts, and cultural workshops.


Our institute preserves the true roots of Beta Israel culture while fostering connections between generations, building cultural pride, and inspiring all who encounter our rich traditions.


Join us in this journey of cultural preservation and artistic excellence, where every workshop tells a story and every creation carries the wisdom of our ancestors.
