About

Beit-El Tibeb Art and Cultural Training Institute is a Jerusalem-based organization that preserves Ethiopian-Israeli heritage through authentic arts and crafts workshops. Led by cultural heritage experts, the institute offers hands-on training in traditional Ethiopian basket weaving, coffee ceremony, textile arts, pottery, and cooking.
