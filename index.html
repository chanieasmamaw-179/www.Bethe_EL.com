About

Beit-El Tibeb Art and Cultural Training Institute is a Jerusalem-based organization that preserves Ethiopian-Israeli heritage through authentic arts and crafts workshops. Led by cultural heritage experts, the institute offers hands-on training in traditional Ethiopian basket weaving, coffee ceremony, textile arts, pottery, and cooking. With small classes (maximum 8 participants) and master craftspeople as instructors, students receive personalized learning experiences and official certificates. Located in Jerusalem's Cultural District, Beit-El also sells Ethiopian organic coffee and handcrafted art products, supporting their mission to celebrate and sustain Ethiopian cultural traditions for future generations.
