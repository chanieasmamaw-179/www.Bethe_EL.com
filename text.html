Key Points:

  • Flexbox (Method 1): Most flexible and modern approach, great for responsive design
  • Grid (Method 2): Excellent for structured layouts with precise control
  • Absolute Positioning (Method 3): Good for overlays and precise positioning
  • Float (Method 4): Traditional method, still works but less flexible

Recommendation: Use flexbox for most scenarios as it's the most versatile and responsive-friendly approach.
